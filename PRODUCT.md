# Product

## Register

product

## Users

TillTally serves small retailers, charity shops, and small e-commerce sellers. Owners and operators use it to turn sales and inventory exports into decisions without learning enterprise business-intelligence tools.

## Product Purpose

TillTally converts CSV data into clear revenue, gross-profit, product-performance, inventory-risk, channel, and weekly-report insights. Success means an operator can import familiar store data, understand what needs attention, and act without building spreadsheets or analytics infrastructure.

## Brand Personality

Concise, trustworthy, and practical. The product should feel focused and professional while keeping operational information easy to scan and use.

## Anti-references

Avoid excessively rounded interfaces, decorative effects that compete with data, generic SaaS templates, card-heavy marketing layouts, and interactions that look polished but do not help users complete real work.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next useful action obvious.
2. Prefer operational clarity over decoration.
3. Show real product states and real business information.
4. Keep repeated workflows fast on desktop and mobile.
5. Earn trust through predictable behavior, clear errors, and honest product claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, sufficient contrast, reduced motion preferences, and layouts that remain usable without horizontal overflow at widths down to 375px.
